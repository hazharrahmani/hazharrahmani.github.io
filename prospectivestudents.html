I am actively looking for graduate and undergraduate students to join my group. Please read the information on this page if you are considering joining my group.

How to start?

The first step for you is likely to get a general idea of my research, which you can do by reviewing our publications and projects.
My research is about developing algorithms and theories for robotics and intelligent systems . Robotics and AI are, of course, interdisciplinary research areas, but we focus on the algorithmic and computational aspects of the problems.
If you love computer science, you will discover that robotics and AI are full of intriguing problems for computer science students.
These problems may not only have potential real-world applications, but they may also involve thought-provoking concepts and intriguing solution approaches.

Graduate Admission

Our department does not have a PhD program. However, we have Master's programs.
Decisions about addmissions is made by the admission committee of our department. These decisions are made largely based on your resume, transcripts, and english test scores. Mentioning potential advisors can positively impact your application. If you're interested in my research, feel free to include my name

Graduate Assistantship

Each year, the department offers a limited number of graduate assistant positions. Not only will the tuition for graduate assistants be waived, but they will also receive a stipend.
In return, graduate assistants will assist instructors in the department with courses, primarily by grading homework assignments and quizzes. Additionally, graduate assistants must choose a thesis-based program and will be assigned to an instructor to advise their thesis.
